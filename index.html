Your Safety First

Electrical Safety

Don't take chances with electricity. Know the warning signs.

Warning Signs

  • Flickering or dimming lights
  • Burning smell from outlets
  • Warm or discolored wall plates
  • Frequent circuit breaker trips
  • Buzzing or sizzling sounds
  • Shocks when touching appliances

Safety Tips

  • Never overload outlets
  • Use GFCI outlets in wet areas
  • Keep electrical devices away from water
  • Don't use damaged cords
  • Test smoke detectors monthly
  • Schedule regular inspections

Emergency? Call Now!

If you smell burning, see sparks, or have a complete power loss, call us immediately. Electrical emergencies can be life-threatening.
